Privacy Policy

Geospatialib is committed to protecting your privacy. Here's how we handle your data:
  • We may collect basic usage data (such as browser type, IP address, and interaction logs) for diagnostic and performance analytics.
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information unless explicitly provided by you (e.g., during sign in).
  • Any authentication is handled securely using third-party services (e.g., Google OAuth), subject to their privacy policies.
  • Your data is stored securely and is never sold or shared with third parties without consent.
  • You have the right to request deletion or access to any data you've shared with us.
